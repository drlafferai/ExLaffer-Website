Alexander Laffer is an academic and writer.

His research focusses on emergent technology, empathy and language. He is a member of the EmotionalAI Lab and is currently working on Project AEGIS, collaborating on the creation of an Ethical Standard for Empathetic Human-AI Partnerships.

He creates digital and design fiction, short stories and poetry to explore speculative realities, technology, and human relationships.

Examples of his work and practice can be found using the links above.

He is a lecturer in Media and Communication, University of Winchester, where he is programme lead for MAs in Media Management and Social Media Management.
